## Core Slogan

**Evolve Everything.**

Secondary line: **Intelligent. Secure. Limitless.**

## Brand Positioning

Helix AI is a sleek, futuristic assistant platform built around adaptive intelligence, secure automation, long-term memory, and connected workflows.

## Logo Usage

- Use the primary square logo for profile images, launch graphics, app cards, and high-impact brand moments.
- Use the transparent header logo in the navigation/header bar.
- Use the favicon files for browser tabs, pinned tabs, app icons, and small UI placements.
- Keep clear space around the logo equal to at least the height of the `AI` letters in the wordmark.
- Use the logo on dark backgrounds whenever possible.
- Avoid placing the neon logo on bright, low-contrast, or heavily saturated backgrounds.

## Typography Direction

Recommended font family: **Inter Display / Inter**.

- Headlines: Inter Display Black or Bold
- UI labels: Inter Medium
- Body copy: Inter Regular
- Technical/caption copy: Inter Medium with increased letter spacing

## Brand Voice

Confident, helpful, capable, secure, advanced, and human-centered. The voice should feel futuristic without becoming cold, and professional without becoming stiff.
