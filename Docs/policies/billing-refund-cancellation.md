This policy is intended to provide clear expectations for users, customers, organizations, developers, and enterprise accounts.

By purchasing, subscribing to, or using paid Helix AI services, you agree to this policy, our [Terms of Use](./terms-of-use.md), [Privacy Policy](./privacy-policy.md), [Data Policy](./data-policy.md), [Security Policy](./security-policy.md), [Acceptable Use Policy](./acceptable-use.md), and any applicable order form, invoice, service agreement, or enterprise agreement.

## 17. Cancellation

You may cancel a paid subscription through the account billing page, organization billing settings, payment processor portal, or another cancellation method made available by SinLess Games LLC.

Cancellation should be reasonably easy to find and complete.

Unless otherwise stated:

- Cancellation stops future renewals.
- Cancellation does not automatically refund past charges.
- You may retain paid access until the end of the current billing period.
- Some features may be disabled immediately if cancellation is connected to abuse, fraud, chargeback risk, security risk, or account termination.
- Add-ons may be canceled with the subscription.
- Usage-based charges incurred before cancellation may still be billed.
- Outstanding invoices remain payable.

For organization or enterprise accounts, only authorized billing administrators or contract contacts may be able to cancel.

## 18. Cancellation Timing

To avoid renewal charges, cancel before the renewal date.

If you cancel after a renewal charge has been processed, the charge may be non-refundable unless required by law, covered by this policy, caused by a confirmed billing error, or approved by SinLess Games LLC.

We recommend canceling at least 24 hours before renewal to reduce the risk of payment processor timing issues, pending charges, or invoice generation.

## 19. Effect of Cancellation

After cancellation:

- Your subscription will not renew.

- Your account may be moved to a free plan, suspended state, read-only state, or limited-access state.
- Paid features may become unavailable.
- Automations may pause or stop.
- API keys may be limited or disabled.
- Marketplace items may become unavailable.
- Storage, memory, vector, and analytics limits may be reduced.
- Organization features may be disabled or restricted.
- Data retention and deletion will follow our applicable policies.

Canceling a subscription does not automatically delete your account or all associated data.

To request account deletion or data deletion, follow the procedures described in the [Privacy Policy](./privacy-policy.md) and [Data Policy](./data-policy.md).
